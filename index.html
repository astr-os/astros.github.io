Why choose AstrOS?

AstrOS is a powerful and user-friendly operating system that offers a seamless experience for both beginners and advanced users. With its intuitive interface and robust features, AstrOS is designed to enhance your productivity and provide a secure computing environment.

  • Simple and Consistent Interface: Powered by GNOME Desktop Environment, AstrOS provides a clean and intuitive user experience.
  • Performance: The AstrOS team works hard to deliver exceptional performance on extremely new hardware as well as computers up to 15 years old.
  • Stable: AstrOS is built on Debian 13 Trixie, ensuring a reliable and stable computing experience.
  • Software Compatibility: Powered by the flatpak ecosystem, AstrOS provides seamless integration with a vast library of applications.
  • Privacy respecting: AstrOS is committed to protecting your privacy and data security.

Powerful for everyone

AstrOS is designed to be the perfect operating system for users of all skill levels, providing a seamless and efficient computing experience.
